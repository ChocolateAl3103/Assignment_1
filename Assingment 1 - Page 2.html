Welcome to HOLOCURE!
               This is a free, unofficial fan game featuring the vtuber talents of Hololive and its surrounding community, with gameplay heavily inspired by Vampire Survivors and Magic Survival. Collect powerful weapons and items and experiment with all kinds of character builds
               to create the ultimate character! Will you be able to save the endless waves of fans, and reach the top rankings?


                                                           In the year 20XX, an unexpected online takeover began. A new group of idols surfaced on the internet, known as virtual idols. People were skeptical at first, but over time, these virtual idols gathered many fans. How-
                                                           ever, one day an evil force came in and took advantage of the fans and their love. The evil force brainwashed the fans, turning them into mindless minions blinded by their own love. The vtubers knew they had to help
                                                           their fans come back to their senses. And thus began their quest to save the fans!


          Gameplay

               In Holocure, players choose a character inspired by Hololive VTubers and battle endless waves of enemies in a top-down, arena-style format. As you defeat enemies, you'll collect XP and items, allowing you to level up and choose new weapons or skills to power up your
               character. Each character has unique abilities, and players can experiment with different builds to maximize their damage or survivability.
               The challenge ramps up as more enemies flood the screen, making quick reflexes and smart decision-making crucial. Between runs, players can unlock new characters, upgrades, and items, adding to the game's replayability. The goal is simple: survive as long as possible
               and climb the leaderboards with the highest score.
